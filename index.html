CV

Xiaojun Lin is an associate professor of health policy and management at the West China School of Public Health and West China Fourth Hospital, Sichuan University and a faculty at the West China Research Center for Rural Health Development and Institute for Healthy Cities, Sichuan University. He is also the member of the Healthcare Evaluation and Organizational Analysis (HEOA) Group.

Dr. Linâ€™s research interests focus on health policy and health economics. His research areas include market structure, health technology diffusion and performance of health care providers. His recent research explores the association of hospital market compeition with inpatient healthcare quality and health technology diffusion. Much of his work involves using the patient discharge data to analyze the patient outcomes based on specific medical conditions. His work is funded by National Nature Science Fundation of China and Chinese Postdoctoral Science Foundation.

Dr. Lin received his PhD degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) China and was previously a visiting scholar at University of Michigan and Saint Louis University.
